I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at [Maastricht Center for Systems Biology (MaCSBio)](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/research/maastricht-centre-systems-biology) under the supervision of [Martina Summer-Kutmon](https://www.maastrichtuniversity.nl/martina.kutmon) since January 2023.

My research interests range from developing new techniques for visualization and analysis of graphs to their application in bioinformatics field. At present, my research is primarily focused on leveraging knowledge graphs on drug repurposing for COVID-19 and its long-term effects (long COVID).
